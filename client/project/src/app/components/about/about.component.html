"Fresh Market" was founded by Kobi Locos in 1901, in a small town near Boston, Massachusetts. Back than, he used to grow and sell all the products- vegetables, fruits, fresh milk and fresh meat. After World War II Locos moved to NYC and opened his first big supermarket. After a decade, there were another 55 branches around the country. Kobi Locos passed away in 1983, and left the supermarkets to his daughter, Jackie Locos. Like her father, she also believed in fresh food and great customer service as key to success. In 2002, the website went up on air for the first time. After few years, Jackie Locos decieded to close most of the supermarket's stores and becoming an online retailer. She decieded to keep the first Fresh Market store that her dad opened: "My dad loved this store, and it's a good memory of him and his big personalty. I decieded to make Fresh Market an online supermarket mostly because of global warming. By selling online, we still supporting local farmers, and give our customers better service and bigger variety of products".
